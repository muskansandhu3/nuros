# Nuros: New Ventures BC Pitch Document

## Page 1: Executive Summary & The Problem

**The Hook:** 
In the next 20 seconds, your voice could reveal sub-clinical neurological or endocrine disorders months before traditional physical symptoms appear. 

**The Problem:** 
The modern healthcare system faces a critical bottleneck in preventative diagnostics, especially in Women's Health. Identifying subtle neuro-motor or endocrine shifts (such as the onset of thyroid dysfunction, perimenopause, or early neurodegenerative decline) often requires months of waiting for specialist visits and expensive blood work. Healthcare systems and private enterprises lose billions annually treating late-stage chronic conditions that could have been identified much earlier. Currently, doctors lack a frictionless, remote, and instantaneous acoustic screening tool capable of tracking these invisible physiological shifts over time.

**The Vision:** 
Nuros is a medical-grade vocal biomarker platform that uses proprietary acoustic AI modeling to help clinicians and telehealth organizations instantly detect and track pre-clinical neuro-motor and endocrine risks through a simple 20-second voice assessment.

## Page 2: The Solution & Product (The "How")

**The Tech:**
Nuros abandons traditional Speech-To-Text (what you are saying) to focus exclusively on Vocal Biomarkers (*how* your vocal cords, respiratory system, and neuro-motor pathways produce the sound). Our neural engine analyzes 100+ micro-acoustic properties—like jitter, shimmer, and formant frequencies—that occur beyond human hearing. 
Using a lightweight, highly optimized CNN Mel-Spectrogram predictor, Nuros assigns dynamic risk scores. Crucially, the AI is explicitly calibrated for distinct Women’s Health life stages (Pregnancy, Menopause, General Baseline). The platform then auto-generates EMR-ready scribe narratives, completely removing administrative burden from the physician.

**Product Maturity:**
Nuros is currently at an **Advanced MVP (Phase 1 Prototype) Stage**. We have built and successfully deployed a fully functional, highly secure Next.js clinical web dashboard and an advanced Streamlit diagnostic portal capable of real-time on-device acoustic feature extraction.

**The Moat:**
Our competitive moat is built on two pillars: Unmatched Domain Specificity and Enterprise-Grade Security. 
1. **The "Vocal Twin" Cryptography:** Nuros tracks "Longitudinal Drift." We establish a cryptographic, AES-256 encrypted baseline of a patient's voice, allowing us to accurately track deviations over time without ever compromising PHI.
2. **Female-Centric Calibration:** Most acoustic AI companies build generalized models. Nuros is specifically targeting the vastly underserved market of women's metabolic and endocrine health scoring.

## Page 4: Business Model & Competition

**Revenue Stream:**
Nuros operates as a high-margin **B2B SaaS and API usage model**:
* **Clinic Tier:** A monthly subscription for independent private clinics based on Monthly Active Patients (MAPs) utilizing the voice triage dashboard.
* **Enterprise API Tier:** Per-API-call licensing for massive EHR providers (like Epic or Jane App) and large remote-health platforms embedding our acoustic scoring directly into their existing apps.

**Competitive Matrix:**
Unlike tech giants focused on hardware (Apple Watch/Oura) or acoustic startups focused on general demographics, Nuros owns the intersection of clinical privacy and specialized preventative screening.

* **Nuros:** Extremely High Clinical Privacy (AES-256), Female-Endocrine Focused, Instant 20-Second Triage.
* **General Voice AI (e.g., Sonde Health):** Broad population focus, lacks our specific calibration for female life-stage metabolics. 
* **Neurological Specialists (e.g., Winterlight Labs):** Heavy focus strictly on late-stage Alzheimer's/Dementia, whereas Nuros detects early auxiliary endocrine/motor drift.

**Go-to-Market (GTM):**
Our first 10 paying customers will be acquired via direct B2B outreach on LinkedIn and private pilot demonstrations. Because the Nuros intake takes exactly 20 seconds, our sales strategy relies heavily on "Live Wow Moments." We will target major Health-Tech and Women's Health conventions, executing live demonstrations that immediately produce clinical scribe narratives—converting early adopters into paid beta pilots on the spot.
