## 3. The Phonetic Stress Protocol 🗣️
**Revealing Sub-Clinical Pathology**
We don't just passively listen to conversational speech; we actively test the glottal muscles.

*   **The Stress Sequence**: The UI instructs the patient to read a specific phonetic pangram: *"The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog."*
*   **The Science**: This phrase forces complex vocal fold transitions, significantly magnifying latent muscle stiffness, trembling, or edema (swelling) associated with endocrine or neuro-motor decline.
*   **Surgical Audio Capture**: Nuros records exactly 20 seconds of audio at a hyper-sensitive **48,000Hz resolution**—a massive leap over standard commercial voice software, ensuring no micro-fluctuation is missed.

## 4. The 3D Silk Waveform & Pipeline 🌊
**Real-Time Transparency**
Once audio is captured, Nuros immediately visualizes its complex backend computation.

*   **The Language Shield**: We apply an aggressive low-pass filter to strip away articulation and accent data. Nuros doesn't care *how* you speak; it measures the physics of *the engine* making the sound.
*   **Fourier Transform Visualizer**: A stunning, interactive 3D topography map is drawn live on-screen, translating unseen soundwaves into a bioluminescent landscape of frequency and amplitude.
*   **Hyper-Sensitized Extraction**: The analysis pipeline rips through the data to capture Jitter (frequency instability) and Shimmer (amplitude perturbation) down to microscopic percentage variances.

## 5. Vocal Twin Delta & Risk Scoring 📊
**Longitudinal Tracking & Clinical Mapping**
A one-off score is good, but tracking physiological change is groundbreaking.

*   **Vocal Twin Technology**: Nuros calculates a unique biometric hash for the voice. It then automatically compares current Jitter/Shimmer data against the patient's historical baseline.
*   **Delta Alerts**: If the system detects a rapid longitudinal degradation exceeding 15%, it flags a "Vocal Twin Alert" indicating potential acute endocrine swelling.
*   **Multi-Modal Risk Matrix**: The backend routes the data to segment risks across:
    *   *Neurological* (Parkinson's, Cognitive Decline)
    *   *Mental Health* (Depression, Chronic Anxiety)
    *   *Respiratory* (COPD)
    *   *Women's Health* (Thyroid issues, Menopause onset)
